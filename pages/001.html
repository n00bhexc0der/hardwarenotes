Introduction

Build your own computer! There are many benefits to building your own computer.

  1. You get hand's on experience learning how a computer works
  2. Expensive then buying one from a retailer
  3. Reliable than buying one from a retailer being that you hand select QUALITY parts and put it togethor yourself.
  4. You can do your own tech support through this material

Requirements

  1. A Computer Case (Something To Put All The Computer Parts Together In). Beware the cheap ones with cheap power supplies they will die in a year
  2. A Mother Board (Everything Will Be Plugged Into The Mother Board)
  3. RAM (Increase If Necessary)
  4. CD/DVD-ROM (If Necessary)
  5. A Hard Disk (Come In Many Different Flavors and Quality Levels, Stores All The Information In Your Computer)
  6. Operating System(Windows XP, Windows Vista, Windows 7, Windows 8, Windows 8.1, Linux, MAC .
